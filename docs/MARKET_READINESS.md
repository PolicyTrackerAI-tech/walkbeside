# Market readiness & company setup — the last-mile checklist

Everything a startup like this needs to be **planted well in the market** that
the strategy docs assume but don't list as a checklist. Each item is tagged
**GATE** (needed before cold outreach or the first hospice contract), **SOON**
(first 90 days), or **LATER**. Most GATE items are **execution with a human**
(lawyer, accountant, designer, expert recruiting) — *not* more strategy. After
this, the planning is complete. The verdict is at the bottom.

---

## A. Corporate & legal setup (the foundation)

- [ ] **Entity** — Delaware C-corp, founder-owned, no mission-lock; EIN, business
      bank account, bookkeeping. **GATE** — can't sign an institutional contract
      without it. *(FINANCE_FUNDRAISING, COMPLIANCE_ADDENDUM)*
- [ ] **ToS + Privacy Policy** rewritten for the free-to-family + institutional
      model (the current ones assume the consumer-pay model). **GATE.**
- [ ] **Contract templates** — the pilot agreement, an institutional MSA/order
      form, a de-identified data-use grant, and a BAA/DPA, drafted with counsel.
      **GATE** (the pilot agreement). *(COMPLIANCE_ADDENDUM)*
- [ ] **Insurance** — get E&O + general-liability quotes now; bind before
      scaling. **SOON.**
- [ ] **Trademark / brand protection** — the "Honest Funeral" mark + a defensive
      domain set. **SOON.**

## B. Trust & credibility (the moat's foundation — most important for this brand)

- [ ] **Named expert reviewers / advisory** — recruit a **licensed funeral
      director, an estate attorney, and a grief clinician** to review the YMYL
      content and appear as **named, credentialed** authors/reviewers. This
      closes the single biggest authority gap the audit found (today the author
      is `Organization`). **GATE for authority.** *(TRUST_SPINE, MARKETING_AUTHORITY)*
- [ ] **Faith/expert content sign-off** — clergy/expert validation of the faith
      profiles before they're treated as authoritative (disclaimers live in the
      meantime). **SOON.**
- [ ] **Neutrality proof, public** — the "we take no money from funeral homes"
      page + methodology + corrections page live and true (no $49 family copy
      contradicting them). **GATE** — the whole pitch rests on this being visible.
- [ ] **Social proof** — an ethical process to gather pilot-family + hospice
      testimonials (consent, no exploitation of grief). **SOON.**

## C. Brand & design

- [ ] **Visual identity / design system** — a logo, palette, and typography that
      signal **calm + trustworthy**, applied to the site and the collateral PDFs
      (the drafts in HOSPICE_COLLATERAL need a look before they're printed).
      **SOON.**

## D. Family-facing operations

- [ ] **Family support SOP** — when a grieving family needs a human: channels
      (the live phone + email), hours, response-time targets, escalation, who
      answers. You're handing families a *service* — they need support behind it.
      **GATE-ish.**
- [ ] **Family journey / UX** — the end-to-end experience once the paywall is
      gone (referral → free tools → advocacy → outcome → after-death). **SOON**
      (overlaps PAYMENT_DECOMMISSION + the PLAN_OF_ATTACK §6B journey).

## E. Go-to-market depth

- [ ] **Competitive battlecard** — positioning + objection handling vs **Empathy,
      Trust & Will, Cake/comparison sites, and funeral homes themselves**, for the
      hospice conversation. **SOON.** *(OPERATING_PLAN appendix has the graveyard
      analysis to build from)*
- [ ] **Pilot success rubric** — explicit thresholds that define a "successful
      pilot" (avg savings, satisfaction ≥ X, ≥ N families, time-to-resolution) so
      the results review isn't subjective. **SOON.** *(SCORECARD, HOSPICE_GTM)*
- [ ] **Hospice onboarding playbook** — training the bereavement coordinator, the
      kickoff, the materials handoff. **SOON.** *(PARTNER_PORTAL_SPEC, HOSPICE_GTM)*

## F. Data, security, measurement

- [ ] **Analytics implementation** — privacy-respecting product analytics
      (visitors, tool uses, email subs), an event taxonomy, the scorecard
      dashboard. Closes the SCORECARD instrumentation gaps. **SOON.**
- [ ] **Data governance & retention** — what we collect / retain / delete, access
      controls for outcomes + family data, tied to the new model (extends the
      existing PRIVACY_RETENTION + SECURITY docs). **SOON.**
- [ ] **Crisis / incident & corrections runbook** — breach response, a
      published-error correction process, a PR-attack response. *"One exposed
      exaggeration undoes the brand"* — this protects the only asset that matters.
      **GATE-ish.** *(TRUST_SPINE corrections page + this runbook)*

## G. Content & authority engine

- [ ] **Editorial calendar + production process** — who writes, the
      expert-review gate, the cadence, with the quarterly Index as the anchor
      event. **SOON.** *(MARKETING_AUTHORITY, FAIR_PRICE_INDEX)*

## H. Fundraising readiness

- [ ] **Pitch deck + data room** — built when you raise (after 1–2 paid contracts
      + traction; the SCORECARD go/no-go is the trigger). **LATER.**

## The verdict: are we ready to attack?

**Strategically — yes.** The thesis, go-to-market, product specs, economics,
legal framework, and metrics are fully planned across 16 documents, with the
[`PLAN_OF_ATTACK.md`](PLAN_OF_ATTACK.md) as the front door. Everything above is
**operational setup + execution-with-a-human + a few SOON planning bits — not
more strategy.** Continuing to plan strategy now has diminishing returns; the
real risk to being "planted well" is that the trust pieces become *real* (named
experts, real data, legal cleared) and that the plan gets executed.

**The minimum to start cold outreach** (the GATE items only): the entity +
ToS/privacy + pilot-agreement template (lawyer); the public neutrality/trust
pages live; a family support SOP; the collateral designed + printed; and Utah
anti-steering cleared. Most are days of work plus the lawyer's lead time, and
they run **in parallel** ([`GO_TO_MARKET.md`](GO_TO_MARKET.md) Phase 0).

**Recommendation:** **stop planning strategy; start the GATE items now** — the
legal pre-flight + entity formation have the longest lead time, so start them
today. Treat the SOON items as the first weeks of execution (most have a home in
an existing spec). **We are ready to attack.**
