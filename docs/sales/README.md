# Hospice Sales Kit

Ready-to-use assets for booking and closing hospice pilots. Built off the value-prop hierarchy in [`../HOSPICE_GTM.md`](../HOSPICE_GTM.md) and the approved family/hospice copy in [`../HOSPICE_COLLATERAL.md`](../HOSPICE_COLLATERAL.md). **Fill in every `[bracketed field]` before sending or printing.**

## The order you'll use them in

| # | Asset | When | What it does |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [`OUTREACH_SEQUENCE.md`](OUTREACH_SEQUENCE.md) | Cold | 3-touch email/call/voicemail/LinkedIn → **booked discovery call** |
| 2 | [`DISCOVERY_SCRIPT.md`](DISCOVERY_SCRIPT.md) | Discovery call | Question script + "listen-for" cues → confirm pain, find the signer, propose a pilot |
| 3 | [`DEMO_SCRIPT.md`](DEMO_SCRIPT.md) | Pitch call | ~10-min live walkthrough of the family experience → their ROI |
| 4 | [`HOSPICE_ONEPAGER.md`](HOSPICE_ONEPAGER.md) | Leave-behind | One-page summary they can forward to the ED |
| 5 | [`PILOT_AGREEMENT.md`](PILOT_AGREEMENT.md) | Close the pilot | Plain-English, free 60-day, self-enrollment, metrics agreed up front |
| 6 | [`ROI_RESULTS_TEMPLATE.md`](ROI_RESULTS_TEMPLATE.md) | After the pilot | Proof sheet on their own families → the paid ask |

## The rules baked into every asset

- **Lead with referral reputation**, then mandate coverage (42 CFR 418.64), then staff hours. Never lead with features.
- **CAHPS = reputation / public reporting, never extra Medicare money.** Stated as a guard in several files; keep it.
- **Free to families. No funeral-home or insurer money, ever. We never steer.** This is what makes the kit ethically and legally handable to a grieving family.
- **Families self-enroll** — no PHI crosses to us, so HIPAA/BAA isn't a blocker (BAA offered only if they ask).
- **Honest timeline:** a pilot can be *signed* in ~90 days, *runs* 60 days; first *paid* contract is realistically 4–6 months out.
- **Grant funding is "being explored," not promised** — don't state it as a committed program to a prospect.
